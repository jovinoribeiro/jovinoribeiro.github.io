What We Do

Two-stepping and line dancing every Friday

We dance

Every Friday night from 7:30PM to 10:00PM at Step By Step Dance Studio, located at 316 N. Palm Canyon Drive, Palm Springs.

We dance two-step, country waltz, shadow, west and east coast swing and line dances.

Admission is $5.00

We teach

We always have a lesson from 7:00PM to 7:30PM before open dancing.

We offer line dance and partner dance lessons (no partner needed)

Lessons are free of charge.

We build community

We produce exceptional country western dance events that cultivate community, volunteerism, and inclusivity within and beyond the LGBTQ+ community.

About Us

Our Mission

We are an organization dedicated to country-western dance instruction and the presentation of country-western dance events for the lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender community and its friends.

Our Vision

We aspire to be the leading all-inclusive country western dance organization in the Coachella Valley.

Our Values

We are an all-volunteer, community supported organization that:

  • promotes the advancement of country western dance through quality dance education.
  • values the contributions of our donors and volunteers while creating an environment of respect and gratitude.
  • celebrates our identities as members and allies of the LGBTQ+ community.

Dance Lessons

New to Country Dancing? Check out our lessons

Lessons

  • Lessons are offered every Friday from 7:00PM to 7:30PM.
  • The first Friday of every month is a line dance lesson. All other Fridays are partner dance lessons including two-step, country waltz, shadow and more.
  • No partner is needed
  • Beginners are especially encouraged to take advantage of lessons twice a week.
  • Smooth leather-soled shoes recommended (cowboy boots!), along with some patience and a desire to have some fun.
